About Us

Comprehensive Hostel Facilities at VDIT for Student Well-being

At VDIT, we understand that the well-being and comfort of our students are of paramount importance to their parents. Thus, we are committed to providing exceptional hostel facilities that offer a nurturing and secure environment for both male and female students.

Accommodation Capacity:

Our state-of-the-art hostel facilities can accommodate a total of 605 students, with the boys' hostel catering to 330 students and the girls' hostel accommodating 275 students.

Hostel Amenities:

  1. Dining Experience:

    Hostel mess ensures the provision of nutritious and hygienic meals, prioritizing the health and dietary requirements of our students. Furthermore, a water purifier system is installed to guarantee access to clean drinking water at all times.

  2. Comfort and Convenience:

    To enhance the living experience, hot water is readily available on every floor, courtesy of our solar heating system. Additionally, uninterrupted power supply is assured through the deployment of generators, ensuring students' comfort and safety even during power outages.

  3. Health and Wellness:

    The campus dispensary is equipped to provide primary medical assistance to students, ensuring prompt attention to their health needs. Our dedicated medical staff ensures that students receive necessary care and support in times of illness or injury.

  4. Connectivity:

    Recognizing the importance of staying connected in today's digital age, we provide 24*7 internet Wi-Fi facility in the hostels, enabling students to engage in academic pursuits and stay connected with their loved ones.

  5. Hydration:

    We prioritize the health and well-being of our students by offering pure drinking water facilities across the campus. Our advanced RO+UV filtering unit ensures that students have access to clean and safe drinking water, promoting their overall health and hydration.

At VDIT, we strive to create a home away from home for our students, where they can thrive academically, socially, and personally. With our comprehensive hostel facilities and unwavering commitment to student welfare, parents can rest assured that their wards are in safe hands, receiving the care and support they deserve.
